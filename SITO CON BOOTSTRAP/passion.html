Football

Practicing and following sports has become a constant in my daily life. Over the years I have found various sports that have attracted me but the one that has always accompanied me is football. It has now become a sort of "positive obsession": in fact, there isn't a day when I don't listen the word "football". The link I have with football is very close and important, in practice we have grown together: already at the age of 5 I started playing with it and still I have not yet "hung up my boots". I think that this sport offers unique sensations and motivate ourselves, it has always been a source of fun but also a moment of freedom from all the rest. In parallel, I developed a passion for football played professionally. It all started when, still very young, I saw the games of the one who made me fall in love with Juventus and probably with football: Alessandro Del Piero. A real champion and captain who has made faithfulness and simplicity his undisputed career. Since then, I have hardly missed a football game and not just of my favourite team. As a good fanatic, I have been participating in a fun event like the Fantasy Football championship with my friends for 3 years now. Every summer and every winter we meet to bid and compete for "psychological fights" with the players of the Serie A championship. Every week some of the players purchased are deployed hoping (and often suffering during the weekend) to defeat opponents getting the most bonuses. As if all this were not enough, I recently started the assistant-coach activity in the children's teams of my country. In these two years I think I have learned some important dynamics of being a coach, an extremely complicated role. It is however a figure that fascinates me a lot although, for now, it has been a simple amusement.

FORMULA 1

In our life, calm and cold-blooded are two important qualities that can help us in many situations. Maybe just because one of my peculiarity is the calm I tried a few years ago to watch Formula 1 and in a short time I was passionate about it. Many consider it an extremely boring sport and, before knowing it, I thought it too. Over time I developed a different concept for this sport: I started to appreciate the great strategies that are at the base and the meticulous work that the pilots and mechanics do every week. It is undeniable that certain tracks are not the best of the show, but many others, where overtaking is the order of the day, repay the expectation of us fans. Of course I immediately supported the Ferrari team although the last few years have been negative for "la Rossa". The man who made me fall in love with this sport, of which I recovered the whole story, was obviously Michael Schumacher. In addition to this legend in more recent times it was Seabastian Vettel who brought me closer to this sport because I was impressed by his guide since he was in RedBull. It is clear that he disappointed everyone after the transition to Ferrari, because unfortunately he failed to win the world championship. As a good fan (even if I'm young) I don't stop supporting her because I believe in this trophy hoping that the old days of the domination of the Maranello team will return.

My Trips

Following the passions of my parents over the years I began to appreciate the many trips that I had the opportunity to make, I managed to admire many archaeological sites and natural landscapes. I am increasingly convinced that one of the most effective ways to learn new things is to visit the places firsthand (and we can say that I'm lucky bacause my Italy isn't second to none). Every time I land in new countries I always appreciate very much knowing the customs and the culture of citizens: the best way to talk about all these characteristics, in my opinion, will always be food. We have confirmation of this here in Italy where in a few kilometers we sometimes find culinary recipes that stand out from family to family. I have had the opportunity to visit countries like Spain, Greece, Turkey, Egypt, Morocco and I intend to continue my discoveries because I am convinced that every place we discover makes us better or in any case gives us more or less important emotions. For a curious boy in the culinary field like me, these experiences are also an opportunity to discover intriguing and completely original flavors.
